About Me

Dr. Shon completed his undergraduate work at the University of California, San Diego. He graduated from New York University College of Dentistry and completed his General Practice Residency at Lincoln Medical Center in Bronx, New York. He has been practicing dentistry since completing his residency and currently works in Orange County and Los Angeles County. He is a member of the American Dental Association and California Dental Association.

Restoratives

Oral Surgery

Non-surgical and surgical extractions including root tips, bone grafting, suturing, and incision drainage

Emergency Dentistry

Management of intraoral/extraoral lacerations, fractured teeth, and odontogenic infection management.
